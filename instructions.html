|| Etch-A-Sketch Version 1.0 Instructions ||

Welcome to Etch-A-Sketch Version 1.0, a browser version of the classic Etch-A-Sketch.

Note: This version only supports aquamarine colored drawings.

Click the "Clear" button if you want to Erase ALL of the aquamarine colored squares.

Click the "Erase" button to manually Erase the aquamarine colored squares.

Click the "Draw" button to resume coloring the squares in aquamarine after you are done erasing.

Be on the lookout for [Etch-A-Sketch Version 2.0 : Red, Green, and Aquamarine] coming out next year.

In addition to letting you draw in Red and Green as well as Aquamarine, Version 2.0 will let you adjust the size of the grid.
